About

I am passionate about exploring, learning, and creating. As a soon-to-be grad, I look forward to working in a passionate and creative environment, with people who enjoy developing meaningful and impactful software.

After I graduate, I hope to work at a company or startup who values collaboration, team work, and are passionate about their mission.

People are very different, I did not know anyone, etc. However, with the right people and awesome adventure plans, I learned how beautful the Bay was. I love the various trails one can explore, like Mnt. Tam. I love the diversity.

Fun facts: I love training, combat sports, lifting, and trail running. I love food, dogs, and grape green tea.
